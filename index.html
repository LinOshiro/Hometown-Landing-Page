Los Angeles, California

The City of Angels

Welcome to my hometown! I was lucky enough to grow up in Los Angeles county in a place called the South Bay. A multicultural, sunny city full of beautiful beaches, laid back people and delicious eats. Growing up in such a large county has given me many happy experiences.

A Few Facts

Los Angeles is the largest city in the state of California and the second most populous city in the United States, after New York City. Los Angeles is known for its Mediterranean climate, its ethnic and cultural diversity, Hollywood Film Industry, Sports Teams and its beaches along the Pacific Ocean. Los Angeles is also the most populous county in the United States with almost 10 million people. Learn More: wikipedia

Activities

Los Angeles is home to many tourist destinations that can be enjoyed by many. From a hiking adventure, to a day at the beach, a theme park adventure, or an evening watching sports. Everyone can find something to enjoy here. Activities: Trip Advisor
